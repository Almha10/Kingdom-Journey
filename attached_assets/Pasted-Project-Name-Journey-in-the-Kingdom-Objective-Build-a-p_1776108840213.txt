Project Name:
"رحلة في المملكة" (Journey in the Kingdom)

Objective:
Build a premium, cinematic, and highly interactive website that showcases Saudi Arabia as a rich cultural, historical, and modern destination. The platform should educate, inspire, and guide both tourists and Saudi locals through an immersive digital journey across the Kingdom.

Core Idea:
The website is not just informational — it is an experience.
Users should feel like they are exploring Saudi Arabia through a digital journey that combines:
- Heritage and history
- Tourism and destinations
- Modern achievements and mega projects
- Cultural identity of each region

--------------------------------------------------

🌙 DESIGN SYSTEM (VERY IMPORTANT)

Theme:
- Dark, elegant, cinematic

Color Palette:
- Primary: Saudi Green gradients (#006C35 → #00A651)
- Secondary: Earthy desert browns (#3E2F1C → #8B6B3E)
- Background: Deep black / charcoal (#0B0B0B / #121212)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) with soft opacity variations
- Accent: subtle glow green highlights

Visual Style:
- Glassmorphism cards
- Soft shadows and glowing edges
- Smooth gradients
- Clean Arabic-first typography
- Luxurious and futuristic look

Allow the user to:

  (3 days, 7 days, 10 days)

- A personalized travel route across Saudi Arabia
